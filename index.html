How To Play
  1. Enemy ships will spawn in waves.
  2. Defeat the enemies to acquire points and money.
  3. Destroy asteroids to find mighty power ups.
  4. Spend your money on upgrades for your ship in the item shop.
  5. Once all enemies have been defeated, you will enter the next level.
  6. Try to score as high as you can!
